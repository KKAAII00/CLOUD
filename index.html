🎮 STORY MODE

LEVEL 1 : THE FOUNDING OF PENANG

Captain Francis Light established Penang in 1786 and transformed it into an important trading port.

LEVEL 2 : STRAITS SETTLEMENTS

Penang joined Malacca and Singapore to form the Straits Settlements under British rule.

LEVEL 3 : WORLD WAR II

Japanese occupation during 1941 changed the history and development of Penang.

LEVEL 4 : MODERN PENANG

George Town became a UNESCO World Heritage Site in 2008 and remains one of Malaysia's most famous destinations.
